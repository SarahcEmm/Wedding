About me

Hi, I’m Holly Franc, a wedding photographer with a passion for capturing love stories in their most beautiful and authentic moments. For me, photography is more than just taking pictures it’s about preserving the emotions, the joy, and the magic of your special day in a way that feels timeless and true to who you are.

With 22 years of experience in wedding photography, I’ve had the privilege of working with incredible couples, each with their own unique love story. My approach is a blend of candid photojournalism and artistic portraiture, ensuring that your wedding photos not only tell the story of your day but also reflect your personalities and the small, intimate moments that make your day unforgettable.

I believe in the power of connection, and that’s why I take the time to get to know every couple I work with. Whether it’s a small, intimate ceremony or a grand celebration, I want to understand your vision so that I can craft images that feel personal and meaningful to you.

When I’m not behind the camera, you can find me hiking or being in nature, always finding inspiration in the world around me. I’m a lover of travel, exploring and music and I bring that sense of curiosity and wonder to every wedding I photograph.

I’d love to hear your story and help capture the moments that will make your wedding day one to remember forever. Let’s create something beautiful together!
